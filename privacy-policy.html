Privacy Policy

This page explains how RevMotion Auto & Marine LLC may collect and use information submitted through this website.

Information collected

RevMotion may collect contact information, seller information, buyer request information, vehicle or marine details, photos, videos, title or ownership notes, pricing goals, delivery needs, and messages submitted through website forms.

How information may be used

  • To review seller submissions and buyer requests.
  • To organize vehicle and marine opportunity information.
  • To contact users about next steps.
  • To coordinate communication when appropriate.
  • To improve website forms, operations, and service organization.

Uploads

Photos, videos, title images, and documents should only be submitted by people authorized to provide them. Do not upload information you do not have permission to share.

Sharing information

RevMotion may share relevant submitted information with involved parties when coordination is requested and appropriate. RevMotion may also refer users to affiliated or related services when requested, such as credit or funding readiness support.

No guarantee

Submitting information does not guarantee listing, buyer interest, sale, delivery, financing, inspection result, or final transaction.

Contact

Questions may be sent through the contact page.
