Private Seller Authorization

No public price formula. Terms are reviewed privately.

RevMotion does not publish a fixed charge or public pricing formula. Every car, boat, motorcycle, RV, trailer, or marine opportunity is different, so seller expectations and RevMotion coordination terms must be reviewed in writing before promotion or matching begins.

How RevMotion handles seller authorization

Seller Goal

The seller tells RevMotion the amount they are hoping to receive and provides condition, title, ownership, location, photos, videos, and supporting documents.

Market Review

RevMotion may review comparable market information, condition, title status, buyer demand, transport needs, and timing before deciding whether the opportunity can be promoted or matched.

Written Authorization

Before RevMotion promotes, posts, matches, or coordinates an opportunity, the seller may be asked to approve a private written authorization explaining the seller expectation and RevMotion role.

Disclaimer: RevMotion may receive compensation for coordination, advertising, buyer search, market review, communication, documentation support, and related services when allowed by written authorization and applicable law. Final title, registration, bill of sale, payment, inspection, transport, and legal documents must be handled properly before any transaction is completed.
Important: This page is not a public price list and does not create an agreement by itself. A seller submission is only a request for review. RevMotion does not guarantee acceptance, posting, buyer interest, final price, sale completion, delivery, title transfer, financing, or inspection results.
