Platform Fees

Clear fees before next steps.

RevMotion may earn a disclosed platform fee, coordination fee, listing support fee, service fee, or success fee depending on the opportunity.

Fee structure

Seller Intake

Basic submission can start free so RevMotion can review vehicle or marine information first.

Buyer Request

Buyers can submit what they are looking for so RevMotion can compare requests with available opportunities.

Coordination Fee

If an opportunity moves forward, RevMotion discloses the platform or coordination fee before next steps continue.

Example

Seller requested net amount$1,000
RevMotion platform / coordination fee$500
Buyer total amount$1,500
The fee should be disclosed before the transaction moves forward. RevMotion does not hide its fee inside the transaction.
