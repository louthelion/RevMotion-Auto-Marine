About RevMotion

Built to connect movement with opportunity.

RevMotion Auto & Marine LLC is a vehicle and marine opportunity coordination platform. The company helps organize information between sellers and buyers for cars, boats, motorcycles, RVs, trailers, and marine equipment.

See How It Works

What RevMotion does

RevMotion collects seller information, buyer requests, vehicle and marine photos, video walkarounds, title status, pricing goals, and next-step details. The goal is to make each opportunity easier to review before anyone wastes time.

Seller Organization

Owners can submit information, condition notes, photos, videos, location, title status, and the net amount they want to receive.

Buyer Requests

Buyers can tell RevMotion what they are looking for, their budget, location, timing, and whether delivery or funding readiness support may be needed.

Platform Coordination

RevMotion helps connect interest, coordinate communication, and disclose any platform, listing, or coordination fee before next steps continue.

Mission, vision, and objective

Mission

To make vehicle and marine opportunities easier to organize, review, and coordinate between sellers and buyers.

Vision

To become a trusted digital coordination platform where people can submit, find, and review auto and marine opportunities clearly.

Objective

To collect the right information upfront so RevMotion can review opportunities faster, communicate better, and reduce confusion.

RevMotion does not own every vehicle or marine item. RevMotion does not guarantee condition, title status, buyer approval, financing, delivery, sale completion, or transaction terms. All information must be verified by the parties before any agreement.
