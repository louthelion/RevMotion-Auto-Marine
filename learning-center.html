Learning Center

Learn before you submit, buy, or coordinate.

The Learning Center is different from articles and blog posts. This page gives practical lessons and preparation steps for sellers and buyers.

RevMotion lessons

Prepare before selling

Before submitting a car, boat, motorcycle, RV, trailer, or equipment, collect the facts that help RevMotion review the opportunity.

  • Year, make, model, trim, VIN or HIN.
  • Mileage or engine hours.
  • Title, lien, loan, payoff, and owner information.
  • Photos, video walkaround, damage notes, and maintenance records.
  • Seller asking price, lowest price, and net amount wanted.

Understand title and lien status

Title and lien information affects whether a buyer can move forward. A clean title, a loan payoff, a missing title, or a lien issue can change the next step.

  • Know whether the title is available.
  • Know whether a bank or lender has a lien.
  • Know the payoff amount if there is a loan.
  • Do not hide ownership problems.

What photos should show

Clear photos help reviewers understand the physical condition before a call or visit.

  • Front, back, driver side, passenger side.
  • Interior, dashboard, mileage, and controls.
  • Engine, motor, tires, trailer, hull, or damage.
  • Use honest lighting and show problems clearly.

Buyer readiness

Buyers should know their budget, payment plan, timeline, delivery needs, and whether they need credit or funding readiness support.

  • Cash or financing.
  • Down payment available.
  • Estimated credit range.
  • Delivery or inspection needs.
  • Must-have features and acceptable condition.

As-is does not mean careless

An as-is opportunity still needs clear information. Buyers should understand condition, title, known issues, and inspection options before moving forward.

Platform fee awareness

RevMotion may earn a disclosed fee for coordination, listing support, or platform service. The fee should be clear before next steps continue.

Learning material is for education and organization only. It is not legal, financial, mechanical, inspection, title, tax, or insurance advice.
