Vehicle & Marine Coordination Platform

RevMotion Auto & Marine

An Uber-style coordination platform for cars, boats, motorcycles, RVs, trailers, and marine equipment.

RevMotion does not own the vehicles or marine equipment. Sellers submit their own information. Buyers submit what they are looking for. RevMotion organizes the opportunity, coordinates next steps, and discloses any platform or coordination fee before the transaction moves forward.

The Flow

How RevMotion works

01

Seller submits

The seller adds the vehicle or marine opportunity with photos, video, condition, title status, and the net amount they want to receive.

02

RevMotion organizes

RevMotion reviews the information and prepares the opportunity for buyer review and next-step communication.

03

Buyer requests

The buyer submits what they need, budget, timeline, delivery needs, and whether credit or funding readiness support may be needed.

04

Coordination moves forward

If there is a match, RevMotion coordinates communication and discloses any platform or coordination fee.
