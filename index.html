The Flow

How RevMotion works

01

Seller sends the file

The seller sends full details, photos, videos, ownership/title information, condition notes, location, and the amount they want to receive.

02

RevMotion reviews and organizes

RevMotion reviews the submission, organizes the media and details, and prepares the opportunity for posting or buyer matching.

03

Buyer submits what they need

The buyer sends the type of car, boat, motorcycle, RV, trailer, or equipment they want, plus budget, location, timing, and delivery needs.

04

Private coordination agreement

Seller-side payment expectations and RevMotion coordination terms are handled privately before RevMotion promotes or coordinates the opportunity.
