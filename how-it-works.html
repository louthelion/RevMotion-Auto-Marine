How It Works

RevMotion organizes the connection.

RevMotion is built around a platform model: the company does not need to own every vehicle or boat to help organize information between sellers and buyers.

The RevMotion process

01

Seller submits the opportunity

The seller provides vehicle or marine details, ownership notes, title or lien status, price goal, photos, videos, and availability.

02

RevMotion reviews and organizes

The information is arranged into a cleaner review package so the opportunity can be understood before buyer communication begins.

03

Buyer submits a request

The buyer sends preferred type, budget, location, payment plan, timeline, delivery needs, and credit or funding readiness needs.

04

Coordination and fee disclosure

If an opportunity matches, RevMotion coordinates next steps and discloses any platform fee, listing support fee, coordination fee, or success fee before moving forward.

05

Parties verify before agreement

Vehicle condition, title status, inspection, final price, payment, delivery, and documents must be verified by the parties before any agreement.

RevMotion does not guarantee sale, buyer approval, financing, delivery, title status, condition, or transaction completion.
